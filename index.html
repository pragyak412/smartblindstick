    • Meeting Doctors, teachers and blind people

    DEFINE

    IDEATE

    The problem faced by blind person

  • Navigation around places
  • Obstacles in path
  • Noise Disturbance
  • Stairs
  • Detecting hot areas
  • Crossing Road

  • Assist blind person during walk
  • Alarm if hurdle is detected within range
  • Material selecion for easy to use
  • Robust and comfortable

    • PROTOTYPE

  • Componet used are ATMEGA16, Sensors and Vibrators
  • Five different sensors with microcontrollers interface are used along with sensors and vibrators
  • ATMEGA16 used as main microcontroller

    • TEST AND VERIFICATION

  • Circuit is successfully checked on software platform
  • Designing is done by taking care of all scenarios
  • Verified by Technical experts and seniors
